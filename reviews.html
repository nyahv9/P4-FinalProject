Bicycle Reviews

Mountain Bike Review

Model: Ascent 27.5" Mountain Bike - 21 Speed

Ascent 27.5" Mountain Bike - 21 Speed is a mountain bike. It has ground-gripping tires, responsive disc brake and smooth rapid shifters. It's frame is also very lightweight and rust resistant, it absorbs impact making mountain trials as smooth as possible.

Rating: 4.9/5

Road Bike Review

Model: Canyon Endurace CFR AXS

The Canyon Endurance CFR AXS is an essentially expensive road bike. It has a build that will maintain comfort and provides a fast, racy ride experience.
The frame is stiff and fast when riding at a fast pace. It also has a light handling, which can provide an easy ride.

Rating: 4.1/5

Hybrid Bike Review

Model: Trek District

The Trek District bike is a hybrid bike, which is purposely built for commuting. It has front and rear lights. It is a quiet and maintence free cruiser. Many rider enjoy it while riding to work or getting groceries.

Rating: 4.3/5

Cruiser Bike Review

Model: Retrospec Chatham 7 Speed

Retrospec Chatham 7 Speed is a prefferable cruiser bike. It has a comfortable saddle that has ultra plush cushioning. It also has relaxed comfort grips on the handles and wide tires that provides a smooth cruising. Riders usually like to use these bikes to cruise near the beach.

Rating: 4.9/5
